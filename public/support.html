Common questions

  • Add or remove a child: open the family board, tap More, then Manage kids.
  • Invite a co-parent: open More, then Share board, and send the invite link to a trusted adult.
  • Reset password: on the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password and follow the email link.
  • Delete a child or account: use the in-app delete controls under More. Deletion is final and removes saved data and avatar files.
